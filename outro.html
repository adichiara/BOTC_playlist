And there you have it—the full countdown of 25 songs, each one clawing its way to the top of this Blood on the Clocktower playlist with a mix of menace, melody, and metaphorical murder.

Let’s be clear: the songs were lovingly (and sometimes questionably) submitted by a group of very opinionated players, each with their own interpretations of the game’s themes. But the rankings? Those were left to an AI with a penchant for lyrical analysis, statistical precision, and zero regard for your personal nostalgia. Think of it as your resident Savant—except it actually gave an answer.

Now, if you’re staring at the list thinking, Wait, why is my favorite song so low? or That one doesn’t even make sense!—congratulations, you’ve just unlocked the true experience of Blood on the Clocktower: feeling absolutely sure you’re right, and still getting voted out.

Is it perfect? No. But neither is your voting strategy.

So cue up the playlist, pour yourself something suspicious, and let these songs soundtrack your next session of lies, loyalty, and last-minute accusations. And hey—if you still think a few tracks got robbed, just remember: even in the game, the truth doesn’t always win.
